School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE)

About School

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 2017 and became the School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCOPE) in January 2019. At present, the School is headed by Dr. CH. Pradeep Reddy. The School has qualified and committed faculty members who offer impressive instruction in a variety of modes, exploring experiential learning approaches and new pedagogical methods in order to provide proper learning to students. The school provides the necessary infrastructure, hardware, and software to support the faculty for their continuous learning and research.

Programs Offered

B.Tech CSE – Core

The fundamental Computer Science engineering program focusing on algorithms, data structures, operating systems, networks, and software development.

B.Tech CSE – Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Specialization in machine learning, neural networks, data science, natural language processing, deep learning, and autonomous systems.

B.Tech CSE – Blockchain

Focus on distributed ledger technologies, smart contracts, Web3, cryptography, and blockchain-based applications.

B.Tech CSE – Cyber Security (CS)

Training in ethical hacking, network security, digital forensics, cryptography, security protocols, and intrusion detection systems.

B.Tech CSE – Data Analytics (DA)

Specialization in big data, predictive analytics, visualization, AI-driven analytics, and business intelligence systems.

B.Tech CSE – Software Engineering (SE)

Focused on large-scale software design, development cycles, DevOps, testing, cloud deployment, and project management.

B.Tech CSBS – Computer Science & Business Systems

Industry-driven program blending computer science with business strategy, analytics, economics, management, and modern computational tools.
